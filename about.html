About Me

My passion is to become a software developer. I have experience working as a QA engineer, which has provided me with valuable insights into the software development process and the importance of ensuring high-quality products. During my time as a QA engineer, I developed strong analytical skills and a meticulous attention to detail, which I apply to every project I undertake. To strengthen my skills and broaden my knowledge, I joined the Village88 web development bootcamp, where I immersed myself in various programming languages and frameworks, learning best practices for building robust applications. This experience not only honed my technical abilities but also ignited my enthusiasm for problem-solving and creating innovative solutions.

In addition to my professional pursuits, I have a range of hobbies that I enjoy. I occasionally play basketball with my acquaintances, which allows me to stay active and connect with friends. However, I spend most of my leisure time playing Valorant, where I can engage in competitive gameplay and work collaboratively with teammates to achieve our objectives. I also have a deep appreciation for chess, a game that challenges my strategic thinking and decision-making skills. Both hobbies contribute to my personal development, helping me improve my focus and mental agility.
